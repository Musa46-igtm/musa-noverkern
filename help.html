How can we help?

How do I install the Nova app?

Head over to the Download page, choose your platform, and follow the simple steps. It’s designed to be smooth — Nova’s got your back.

Is Nova free?

Nova’s core experience is totally free. Some advanced features might come with a premium plan, but staying connected doesn’t cost a thing.

Where do I change my settings?

Easy. Just navigate to Settings from the main menu. There you can update your theme, language, account details, and more.

How do I contact support?

You can reach out directly through our contact form on the website, or email support@novakern.com. We usually reply within 24 hours.
